Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CalFit ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. By using CalFit, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name you choose. Authentication is handled by our backend provider (Supabase).

Community content. If you use the community feature, the posts, comments, and images you choose to share are stored on our servers and visible to other signed-in members.

Health and activity data. Food logs, weight entries, exercise records, and body measurements you enter are stored locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers.

Food photos. When you use AI photo recognition, the photo you take is sent for analysis to generate nutrition estimates. Photos are processed for that purpose and are not used to identify you.

Usage data. We keep simple on-device counts of feature usage (for example, daily limits) to operate the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To operate the community feature you choose to participate in.
  • To provide nutrition estimates and the AI assistant.
  • To calculate your calorie targets and show your progress.
  • To enforce subscription access and daily usage limits.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — account authentication and storage of community content.
  • Google (Gemini) — AI photo recognition and the nutrition assistant. Food photos and questions are processed by this service.
  • USDA FoodData Central — public food and nutrition database used for food search.
  • Apple — subscription purchases are processed by Apple; we do not receive your payment details.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Health, food, weight, and exercise data stay on your device and are removed if you delete the app. To delete your account and associated community content, contact us at the email below and we will remove your data.

5. Children's Privacy

CalFit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

6. Medical Disclaimer

CalFit is a general wellness and fitness tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a qualified professional for health decisions.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
